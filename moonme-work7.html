LAGOM Snacks

2020.8 / Client: LAGOM

LAGOM is a cafe located in downtown. The owner is a very lovely couple who are serious about coffee and hope to become a world-class barista. They wanted us to design the package of the snacks they cooked and sold.
The essential part of our design was to convey a relaxed feeling through the package, which is cosistent with the owners' philosophy of life.
Considering that they needed to control costs of packages and they'd already ordered a set of simple white boxes, we decided to design a stickboard for them with various patterns, so it could be quite flexible for them to pick up different stickers to decorate the packages. It was an easier, faster as well as cheaper way for them to make packages for their snacks.
Besides, with the package design process, we also made cartoon images for them, which was inspired by the male owner's poem, the main character in which was a lazy hippo.
