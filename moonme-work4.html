UNO SPACE

2021.2-3 / Coursework

This is a relatively comprehensive design project, aiming to create a distinctive brand identity for a coffee shop specifically with the theme of UNO game, which was one of the global games that ThunderFire Studio planned to promote.
We used cubes, very basic elements to represent the interest of UNO games by combining different cubes to create various forms. To convey our ideas more clearly, I added a lot of motion to these cubes in the concept promotion clip, vividly expressing the endless fun of UNO game.
